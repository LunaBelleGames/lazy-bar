About the Hole

Floating in the void between star systems, Lazy's Lost Soul Watering Hole isn't just a bar—it's a sanctuary for the weary, the broken, and the searching.

Here, spacers, drifters, and lost souls gather to share stories, forget their pasts, and occasionally find their futures. The drinks are strong, the company is questionable, and the jukebox only plays songs from dead civilizations.

Welcome to the edge of nowhere. Pull up a stool.
